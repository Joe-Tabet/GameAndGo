Discription

In "Alien: Isolation," fear takes center stage as Amanda Ripley, daughter of Ellen Ripley, embarks on a perilous mission 15 years after her mother's disappearance. Set in the decommissioned trading station Sevastopol, the game unfolds a survival horror narrative against a backdrop of panic and mistrust.

Underpowered and underprepared, players must scavenge, improvise, and outsmart a dynamic and reactive Alien, ensuring survival in a world teeming with mortal danger. Hacking systems, crafting items, and making strategic choices become paramount in facing the ever-present threat.

The storyline intertwines with the mystery of Ellen Ripley's ship, the Nostromo, and the recovered flight recorder at Sevastopol. What begins as Amanda's quest for truth swiftly transforms into a tale of betrayal and an unknown menace lurking in the shadows.

Immersed in the detailed setting of Sevastopol, players encounter a rich cast of characters within a world scarred by fear. The game captures the essence of survival horror, where the line between life and death blurs, and every decision becomes a critical choice in the desperate battle for survival.
